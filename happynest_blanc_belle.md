## About HappyNest

Nestled in the serene embrace of Sohna, HappyNest — Blanc Belle — stands as a testament to luxury and elegance. Its name draws from its immaculate white interiors and a gleaming facade that shines like a pearl in the sun. This is more than just a home; it is an opulent retreat that beckons weary souls seeking refuge from the mundane.

A haven for weekend getaways, the property delights every visitor with its inviting pool, bubbling jacuzzi, sprawling lawns, and outdoor games. Blanc Belle's charm lies not only in its seclusion but also in its accessibility — close enough to urban centres, yet far enough to truly unwind. Furry companions are welcomed with open arms, making this the ultimate destination for unforgettable getaways.

**What makes HappyNest special:**

- Opulent and thoughtfully arranged interiors — a haven of space and luxury
- Outdoor pool accompanied by a soothing jacuzzi
- Extensive lawn with a gazebo, ideal for social gatherings and celebrations
- Quintessential location, away from the bustling urban clamour
- Diverse selection of indoor & outdoor games for all ages
- Curated experiences — cosy bonfires, sizzling BBQ nights, and more
- Senior citizen friendly and pet friendly

## Location & Nearby Attractions

HappyNest is nestled amidst the verdant embrace of the Aravalli Hills in Sohna — a charming town known for its rejuvenating hot springs, scenic landscapes, and mystical legends.

**Things to do nearby:**
- Visit the famous Sohna Hot Springs
- Explore Damdama Lake
- Visit the ancient Shiva Temple
- Take a cultural tour of nearby villages
- Go trekking in the scenic Aravalli Hills
- Explore Badkhal Lake
- Discover historical sites — Thakur Raghunath Temple, Rani Mahal

**Getting Here:**
- Indira Gandhi International Airport — 55 km
- New Delhi Railway Station — 60 km
