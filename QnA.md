# Farmhouse Booking – Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## 1. What are the check-in and check-out timings?

* **Check-in:** Around **2:00 PM**
* **Check-out:** By **12:00 PM (next day)**

This timing helps the staff clean and prepare the property properly for the next guests.

---

## 2. What are the pool timings?

The official pool timing is usually until **10:00 PM**.

In some situations, guests may use it slightly later, but this depends on safety and management discretion.

---

## 3. Why is the pool closed at night?

For safety reasons:

* Visibility becomes low at night
* Drinking near the pool can become risky

We strictly encourage:

### **“Don’t Drink and Swim”**

---

## 4. Can we stay awake and eat late at night?

Yes, guests often stay up late and enjoy late-night meals.

To make things easier:

* Food may be shifted to a **buffet setup** after late evening hours.

---

## 5. Is loud music allowed?

Yes, outdoor music is allowed until approximately **10:30 PM**.

After that:

* Loud music must be moved **indoors**
* Indoor music and celebrations are allowed as long as outdoor disturbance is avoided

This helps maintain peace for nearby properties and neighbors.

---

## 6. Can we party indoors after late night hours?

Yes. Indoor celebrations are allowed after outdoor sound restrictions begin.

You may continue enjoying inside the property while keeping outside noise controlled.

---

## 7. Is hookah allowed?

* **Outdoor hookah:** Allowed
* **Indoor hookah:** Not allowed

### Why?

Indoor hookah smoke can damage or clog air-conditioning systems.

If hookah is used indoors:

* Additional cleaning/service charges may apply.

---

## 8. Can we use the outdoor park/open areas freely?

Yes, guests can enjoy the outdoor and park areas freely during their stay while following property rules.

---

## 9. Are there any important pool safety rules?

Yes. Guests are requested:

* Not to jump or dive recklessly into the pool
* To follow all safety instructions around the pool area

Safety is a top priority for all guests.

---

## 10. Are there extra charges for property damage or rule violations?

Yes. Any damage caused to property equipment, especially AC systems due to indoor smoking/hookah, may result in additional service or repair charges.

---

## 11. Why are some rules strict after late night hours?

The rules are mainly for:

* Guest safety
* Noise control
* Respecting neighboring properties
* Maintaining the property properly for everyone’s comfort

---

## 12. Who can we contact if we have questions during our stay?

The property manager or support staff will assist you during your booking period for any concerns or guidance.

---
